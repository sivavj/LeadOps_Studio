Frequently Asked Questions

Is Google Ads cost included in your pricing?

No. Google Ads budget is paid directly to Google. Our pricing covers setup, management, optimization, and lead tracking.

Do you guarantee first position on Google?

Google rankings depend on competition and location. We do not guarantee positions, but we focus on improving visibility and generating real enquiries.

How soon can I expect enquiries?

Results depend on your business type, competition, and budget. Some businesses start receiving enquiries within the first few weeks.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Our plans are month-to-month. You can stop anytime if it's not a good fit.

Who is this service best suited for?

Our services are best for local service businesses that depend on calls, WhatsApp enquiries, and bookings.

Do you work outside Tamil Nadu?

Yes. While we primarily serve Tamil Nadu, we also work with businesses across India.
