The Business Challenge

A premium property developer in Tiruppur was running Facebook lead generation ads. While lead volume was high, the quality was extremely poor. More than 80% of leads provided fake numbers, invalid email addresses, or had budgets under ₹25 Lakhs when the project entry price was ₹85 Lakhs. Sales agents wasted hours calling cold files instead of closing warm prospects.

Our Optimization Strategy

LeadOps Studio restructured their funnel from broad social interest to deep-funnel search intent:

  • Intent-Based Paid Ads: Moved ad spend from Facebook broad demographics to Google Search high-intent keywords (e.g. "luxury villas in Tiruppur", "3BHK apartments in Kangeyam").
  • WhatsApp Qualification Bot: Required lead targets to interact with our conversational bot to confirm mobile validity, property timeline, and minimum budget capacity.
  • CRM Routing Filter: Set up workflow rules to only sync leads who verified budgets over ₹80 Lakhs to the active agent dashboard.

Execution & Impact

Unqualified leads were automatically filtered out at the chat stage, keeping the developer's CRM clean. The cost per *qualified* lead dropped by 70%, and sales agents reported that prospects were pre-educated about price constraints before the first call, resulting in a 3x higher deal close rate.
