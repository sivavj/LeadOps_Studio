The Ultimate WhatsApp Automation Guide

Published: July 8, 2026 | By LeadOps Developer Team

WhatsApp is the most popular communication channel in India. Utilizing official automations lets you connect with local customers instantly, scaling bookings and trust.

1. Understanding the WhatsApp API

Unlike the basic WhatsApp Business app, Meta's developer API allows third-party tools to connect, sending templated reminders, custom quick-reply buttons, and qualifies lead cards automatically. Read the details on our WhatsApp Automation Service Page.

2. Building an Automated Intake Pipeline

When a user clicks your website link, trigger an opt-in message. The AI assistant prompts them for name, city, and interest area, qualifies the budget category, and syncs data to spreadsheets.

3. Reminders & Broadcast Compliance

Always use approved templates for outbound reminders. Triggering reminders 24h and 1h before booking slot minimizes client no-shows and maps cancellations.
