Website Mistakes That Lose Customers

Published: July 8, 2026 | By LeadOps UI/UX Designer

If your website receives impressions but no phone calls or WhatsApp messages, it is not built to convert. A pretty page that sits idle is an expensive digital business card.

1. Poor Mobile Optimization

Over 70% of local traffic searches occur on phones. If navigation buttons are too small, text requires zooming, or layout sheets fail, visitors leave instantly. Read our guide on the Website Development Service Page.

2. Confusing Call-To-Action (CTA)

Visitors shouldn't guess what to do next. Having multiple competing buttons (like "Read More", "Learn Us", "Gallery") dilutes intent. Focus on a single primary CTA: "Book a Free AI Audit".

3. Slow Load Speed

If a website takes more than 3 seconds to render, bounce rates spike by over 50%. Optimize images, remove heavy external plugins, and write semantic HTML layout tags.
