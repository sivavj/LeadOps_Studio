Local SEO Checklist

Published: July 8, 2026 | By LeadOps Local SEO Specialist

Local SEO targets buyers within 10–15 kilometers of your office. Use this structured checklist to ensure your listing ranks in top-3 Google Maps search results.

1. Claim & Optimize Google Business Profile

Set accurate hours, list specific services, write description content loaded with local keywords, and upload photo logs weekly. Check our full setup service in our Local SEO Service Page.

2. Map Citation Consistency (NAP)

Ensure your Name, Address, and Phone details are formatted identically across maps, directories, and social pages.

3. LocalBusiness Schema Markup

Embed structured JSON-LD coordinates, served postal codes, and category properties directly into your website's header tag.
