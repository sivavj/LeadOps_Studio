Frequently Asked Questions

Comprehensive answers covering AI automation, Google maps rankings, WhatsApp API integration, local SEO, and advertising strategies.

What is AI Business Automation, and how does it help local clinics or shops? ▼

AI Business Automation uses smart algorithms and chat interfaces (like WhatsApp API or website bots) to automatically handle lead interactions. For clinics, it answers FAQs about pricing, doctor availability, location coordinates, and books appointments 24/7. This saves your admin team hours and prevents lead drops.

How does WhatsApp Automation work? Do I need a special business phone number? ▼

Yes, we use the official Meta WhatsApp Business API. You can use your existing business phone number, but it must be verified. The automation links this API key to our AI conversational engine, allowing the system to send automated templates, verify user details, and route qualified queries to your mobile device.

Do you manage Google Business Profile (GBP) optimization? ▼

Absolutely. We handle complete GBP setup, keyword optimization in the business description, category alignment, weekly photo updates, map location mapping, and automated customer review generation strategies to push your business into Google's top-3 Local Pack.

What is the difference between Local SEO and Standard SEO? ▼

Standard SEO targets generic, global keywords. Local SEO targets geographical phrases like "Website Development in Tiruppur" or "Dentist near me". It optimized your digital footprint so customers within 10–20 kilometers can locate your physical office easily.

Are Google Ads budgets included in your monthly packages? ▼

No. Your advertising budget is paid directly to Google. Our monthly management fees cover campaign setup, negative keyword tracking, copywriting, landing page design, conversion code setups, and monthly reporting.

How fast do your website pages load on mobile devices? ▼

We target a Lighthouse Performance Score above 95/100. By avoiding heavy framework structures, writing clean CSS rules, and using lightweight Tailwind configurations, our sites load in less than 1.5 seconds, even on standard mobile 4G networks.

Do we get a custom CRM for tracking customer leads? ▼

Yes, on our higher plans, we link all form submissions and WhatsApp numbers into an active Customer Relationship Management (CRM) dashboard where you can see the lead stage, record notes, set reminders, and trace traffic source attribution.

What happens in the Free AI Growth Audit? ▼

Our technical team reviews your current website loading speed, mobile responsive design, Google Business Profile ranking signals, local keyword gaps, and competitor density. We deliver a screen-recorded video report directly to your WhatsApp with actionable tips.

Can AI automate appointment reminders? ▼

Yes. The workflow triggers automated notification rules. When a lead selects a date, they receive a booking confirmation card on WhatsApp, followed by auto-reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before the scheduled consultation to minimize no-shows.

Do we need to write blog posts ourselves? ▼

No, we program custom AI writing systems specifically trained on your industry guidelines to generate highly educational, keyword-rich SEO blog content. Our editorial team reviews every text before publishing.

Is your system secure? How do you protect patient/client privacy? ▼

Data security is our primary focus. All chat channels use official encrypted APIs from Meta. Lead databases are logged securely in compliance with local privacy frameworks and standard CRM security protocols.

How do you track call leads? Do you record calls? ▼

We set up specific tracking numbers (call forwarding codes) that display dynamically to paid search traffic. When a client calls, the code redirects the line to your cell, logging the timestamp and Google ad campaign origin.

What happens if we want to change or update text on our website? ▼

We provide ongoing web maintenance and minor content upgrades in our monthly packages. Just ping us on WhatsApp, and updates are processed within 24 business hours.

Do you offer SEO services in Tamil? ▼

Yes, we write localized ad copies and GBP update posts in Tamil to connect directly with regional client demographics across Tiruppur, Coimbatore, and Erode.

How do we get started? How long does setup take? ▼

Simply fill out our Free AI Growth Audit form. We'll deliver your diagnostic video report within 24 hours. The entire system setup (website rebuild, GBP optimization, WhatsApp API routing) takes 10 to 14 days.
