Meta Business API & WhatsApp Business API Data Handling

As part of our automation services, we access and process data through Meta's official APIs. The following describes how we handle this data:

  • Access Tokens: We securely store Meta API access tokens (System User Tokens and Page Access Tokens) required to operate automation on your behalf. These tokens are encrypted at rest and never shared with third parties.
  • Message Data: WhatsApp messages sent and received via the API are processed for automation purposes only. We do not read, mine, or use message content for advertising or any purpose unrelated to the services you have engaged us for.
  • Phone Numbers: Customer phone numbers collected through WhatsApp opt-ins are used solely for the purpose of sending approved business messages (marketing, utility, or authentication templates) and are not sold or shared.
  • Webhook Data: Delivery receipts, message status updates, and other webhook payloads from Meta are processed to provide analytics and ensure proper functioning of automation workflows.
  • Business Account Information: Your WhatsApp Business Account ID (WABA), phone number ID, and Facebook Page information are stored to maintain API connectivity and manage your automation setup.
  • Template Messages: Pre-approved message templates submitted to Meta for approval are stored in our systems for campaign management purposes.

Meta Platform Terms Compliance

We comply with Meta's Platform Terms, Developer Policies, and WhatsApp Business Policy. Specifically:

  • We only access Meta API data that is necessary for providing our contracted services to you
  • We do not sell, license, or purchase any data obtained from Meta or its users
  • We do not use Meta API data to build or augment user profiles for advertising purposes beyond the services you have engaged us for
  • We do not transfer Meta API data to any data broker, advertising network, or other third party not directly involved in delivering our services
  • We delete Meta API data when it is no longer needed for the services, or upon termination of our agreement with you, whichever comes first
  • We maintain appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect all data received through Meta APIs
  • We ensure that all WhatsApp messages sent through our automation comply with Meta's messaging policies, including obtaining proper opt-in consent from recipients before sending marketing messages

Data Retention for Meta API Data

We retain data obtained through Meta Business API and WhatsApp Business API as follows:

  • Message logs: Retained for up to 90 days for analytics and troubleshooting purposes, then automatically deleted
  • Access tokens: Retained only while the service agreement is active; revoked and deleted upon termination
  • Customer contact data: Retained only for the duration of the automation campaign or service agreement, unless you instruct us otherwise
  • Webhook event data: Processed in real-time and retained for up to 30 days for operational purposes

Upon termination of services, we will delete all Meta API data within 30 days unless legally required to retain it.

Your Rights Under Indian Law

Under Indian data protection laws (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023), you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, including any data processed through Meta APIs
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal information, including Meta API data, subject to legal and contractual obligations
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time, including revoking Meta API access permissions
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving WhatsApp marketing messages at any time by replying "STOP" or contacting us directly

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.
